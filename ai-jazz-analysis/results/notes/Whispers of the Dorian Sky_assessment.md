## Overall Impression

The chords have proper extensions and music. The melody is simple but effective. However, as the song progresses, the coherence of the overall ensemble falls extremely quickly, till the end where it doesn't really make any sense.

---

## Axis-by-Axis Scores

### 1. Harmonic Authenticity — 2/5

Simple chord extensions from the start with the melody being fine. The chords start to wander, but somehow it feels fine to the untrained ear. But at **0:47**, it goes in a completely different direction, with random chords starting to play.

---

### 2. Swing Feel & Microtiming — 2/5

The swing starts consistent but later on just completely falls apart at **0:32**. Additionally, the percussion doesn't follow the same swing that the rest of the music is following.

---

### 3. Improvisational Coherence — 1/5

The melody starts with coherence and simplicity, but later devolves into random fast runs that do not fit the chords at all. Seen at **0:55**.

---

### 4. Idiomatic Jazz Vocabulary — 2/5

The random melodies do have chromatic tones and classic jazz melodies, they just do not fit the slow, modal context of the piece.

---

### 5. Ensemble Interaction — 2/5

The ensemble interacts with each other consistently throughout the piece, but it doesn't try to complement each other to expand the timbre and sound. Again towards the end, it falls apart and the pitch of each instrument does not follow each other.

---

### 6. Formal Structure — 1/5

There is essentially no structure to the piece, with random chords and random movements, and absolutely zero repetition.
